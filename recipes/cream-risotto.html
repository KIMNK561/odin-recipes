Cream Risotto

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice (or other risotto rice)
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable stock, kept warm
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons butter and 1 tablespoon olive oil in a pan; saut√© chopped onion and minced garlic until translucent.
  2. Add 1 cup Arborio rice and stir to coat it with the butter and oil for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Deglaze the pan with 1/2 cup white wine (optional) and let it evaporate.
  4. Gradually add warm stock (1 ladle at a time), stirring constantly, allowing the rice to absorb it before adding more.
  5. Repeat until the rice is tender and creamy, about 18-20 minutes.
  6. Reduce heat and stir in 1/2 cup heavy cream to enrich the risotto.
  7. Add 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until melted and combined.
  8. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with chopped parsley (optional) and extra Parmesan if desired.
