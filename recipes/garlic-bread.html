Garlic-Bread

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette or loaf of bread
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the baguette diagonally into thick pieces, or cut in half lengthwise.
  3. In a bowl, mix softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the bread slices or halves.
  5. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top (optional).
  6. Wrap the bread in aluminum foil for softer garlic bread, or leave it open for crispy edges.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until the butter melts and the bread is golden.
  8. Remove from the oven and unwrap if using foil.
  9. Garnish with extra parsley if desired.
