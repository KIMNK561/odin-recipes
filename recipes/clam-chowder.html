Clam-Chowder

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 cups clams (fresh or canned), chopped (reserve the juice if using canned)
  • 2 cups clam juice or seafood stock
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 2 slices bacon, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley or chives (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook 2 slices of chopped bacon in a large pot until crispy; remove and set aside, leaving the fat in the pot.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons butter to the pot, then saut√© diced onion and celery until soft.
  3. Stir in 2 tablespoons flour, cooking for 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
  4. Gradually add 2 cups clam juice or seafood stock, whisking to avoid lumps.
  5. Add diced potatoes and cook until tender, about 10-15 minutes.
  6. Stir in the reserved clams and their juice (if using canned), cooking gently to warm through.
  7. Add 1 cup heavy cream, stirring to combine; do not boil.
  8. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with crispy bacon and fresh parsley or chives if desired.
